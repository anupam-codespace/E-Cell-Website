About TechFlare

TechFlare 2025, the premier event of Barak Valley Engineering College (BVEC), hosted by the Entrepreneurship Cell under the banner of IIC-BVEC, stands as a distinguished platform celebrating innovation, collaboration, and entrepreneurship. Bringing together the BVEC Code Club, Robotics Club, CAD Design Club, and E-Cell BVEC, TechFlare 2025 offers a unique convergence of talent where ideas meet execution. With flagship events such as Hackathons, Pitching Contests, Robotics Challenges, and Design Competitions, the summit fosters creativity, critical thinking, and future-ready skills.
