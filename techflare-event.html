TechFlare 2025

15th Oct 2025 • BVEC Main Campus • Hosted by E-Cell BVEC

Overview

TechFlare 2025 is the flagship mega event of Barak Valley Engineering College (BVEC), proudly hosted by the Entrepreneurship Cell of BVEC under the banner of Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC-BVEC). This one-of-a-kind celebration of innovation, skills, and collaboration brings together the brightest minds of the campus and beyond, igniting a spark of creativity and entrepreneurship that defines the next generation of leaders and innovators. What makes TechFlare 2025 truly unique is its collaborative spirit. Four dynamic clubs of BVEC—BVEC Code Club, Robotics Club, CAD Design Club, and E-Cell BVEC—have come together to craft a platform where innovation meets execution, and ideas come alive. This synergy ensures participants learn, compete, and grow in multiple domains of technology and entrepreneurship, scaling mindsets and nurturing future-ready skillsets.
