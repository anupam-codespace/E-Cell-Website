TECHFLARE'25

15th Oct 2025 • BVEC, Main Campus

About TechFlare

TechFlare 2025 is the flagship mega event of Barak Valley Engineering College (BVEC), proudly hosted by the Entrepreneurship Cell of BVEC under the banner of the Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC-BVEC). A celebration of innovation and collaboration featuring top talent and interdisciplinary challenges. Four major clubs — BVEC Code Club, Robotics Club, CAD Design Club, and E-Cell BVEC — come together to create this platform for competition and learning. Ignite your creativity, push boundaries, and join us in shaping the future of technology and entrepreneurship.
